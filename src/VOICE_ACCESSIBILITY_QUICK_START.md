# Voice Accessibility - Quick Start Guide

## ‚ö° Quick Start (30 seconds)

### For Visually Impaired Users:

1. **Enable Voice Mode**
   - Click the floating microphone button (bottom-right corner)
   - Or click "Enable Voice Mode" in the blue notice box

2. **Allow Microphone**
   - Your browser will ask for permission
   - Click "Allow"

3. **Listen and Speak**
   - Wait for the welcome message
   - Say **"Ready"** to begin
   - Follow the spoken instructions
   - The system will guide you through everything!

That's it! The system will automatically:
- ‚úÖ Tell you what to say
- ‚úÖ Listen to your voice
- ‚úÖ Fill in the forms
- ‚úÖ Move to the next step
- ‚úÖ Confirm before submission

## üéØ Pro Tips

### For Best Results:
1. **Speak clearly** (but normally)
2. **Pause between digits**: "One. Two. Three." ‚úÖ (not "one-two-three" ‚ùå)
3. **Use headphones** to hear instructions better
4. **Quiet place** helps (but not required)
5. **Wait for beep** before speaking

### If You Make a Mistake:
- Say **"Repeat"** to hear again
- Say **"Clear"** to start field over
- Say **"Back"** to go to previous page
- Say **"Cancel"** to exit and use keyboard

## üìû Need Help?

### Common Questions:

**Q: Do I need to speak loudly?**  
A: No, speak at normal volume. Your regular speaking voice is fine.

**Q: Can I go back if I make a mistake?**  
A: Yes! Say "Back" to go to previous page, or "Repeat" to hear instructions again.

**Q: What if I can't remember the commands?**  
A: Just listen! The system tells you exactly what to say at each step.

**Q: Can I switch between voice and typing?**  
A: Yes! You can use both at the same time. Voice mode is just an option.

**Q: Is my voice data safe?**  
A: Yes! All processing happens in your browser. Nothing is recorded or sent anywhere.

**Q: What if voice mode stops working?**  
A: You can always use the keyboard and mouse to fill forms normally.
